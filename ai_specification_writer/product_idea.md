# CarPool Connect Product Idea

## Vision
CarpoolConnect helps companies save money and reduce carbon emissions by matching employees for shared daily commutes. Colleagues ride together instead of driving alone — cutting costs, pollution, and parking demand while building workplace connections.

## Users
- **Corporate employees**: Daily commuters seeking cheaper rides, less driving stress, and friendly conversations with coworkers
- **HR/Facility managers**: Track employee satisfaction, sustainability progress, and parking optimization metrics
- **Sustainability officers**: Need clear measurements and reports proving environmental impact reduction

## Key Features
- **Smart matching**: Automatically finds coworkers with similar routes (within 2 miles) and schedules (±30 minutes flexibility)
- **Company login**: Uses existing corporate accounts so employees access instantly without new passwords
- **Impact tracking**: Shows live CO2 savings and creates monthly reports for leadership presentations
- **Parking rewards**: Reserves prime spots for verified carpool groups and optimizes total parking usage
- **Savings proof**: Calculates money saved per employee and proves ROI for company transportation programs
